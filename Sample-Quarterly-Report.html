Where this record stands

Your state (New York): all current-year requirements are met. The IHIP, the four quarterly reports, the 900-hour minimum, the full subject list, and the grade-4 assessment are each satisfied.

Beyond New York, the same record also satisfies Massachusetts (an approvable education plan), Pennsylvania (an evaluator-ready portfolio + grade-appropriate testing), and South Carolina (Option 3, association path).

One difference to know: Rhode Island's 1,080-hour ceiling is the highest in the country. At 947 hours you are at 88% of it. New York does not require this — it is flagged only so you'd know the single item to top up if you ever moved to RI.

Net: this family could relocate to any U.S. state and remain substantively compliant, with RI's extra hours the only gap.
